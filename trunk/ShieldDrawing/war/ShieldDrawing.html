How to use the application

The application will try to understand a given sentence of Blazon in order to generate an image of the shield that the Blazon represents.

To generate a shield, simply enter your sentence of Blazon into the text box and click the "Draw Shield" button.

The Blazon you enter must be valid and adhere to the restrictions as listed below. If the application can not understand the sentence, or encounters and error, an error message will appear.

How add a charge to the database

If a charge is to be placed on your shield, it must be stored in the database. When you request a charge that is not in the database, the application will give a message asking you to add it.

To add the charge you must specify:

  • What the charge is. e.g. Lion, Tree, Chuck Norris.
  • What attitude the charge has. e.g. Rampant, Volant.
  • What tincture the charge is. e.g. Or, Gules, Proper.
  • The URL where the image is located.

You may also specify:

  • The way the charge is facing. e.g. Reguardant.
  • Up to 5 body parts with a different tincture.
    e.g. Langued Gules, Armed Argent.

Once you have entered the charge details, click the "Add Charge to Database" button. The charge will then be added to the database to be retrieved at any time.

Application Limitations

Due to the time contraints of the project there are a number of limitations as to which Blazon sentences the application can parse.

The parts of Blazon that the application can't do are:

  • Counter-changing.
  • Marshalling.
  • Line Variations. e.g. Embattled, Wavy
  • Placing charges upon others. e.g. "...on a chief azure a roundel or"
  • Multiple types of charge.
