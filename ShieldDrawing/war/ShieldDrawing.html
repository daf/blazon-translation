The Application

For those of you not familiar with Blazon, I have provided some example sentences for you to try, otherwise enjoy the application.

There are some limitations to what parts of Blazon the application can deal with, please see here for details.

If the application gives an error about a charge not being present in the database then please take a look at the FAQ.

Example Sentences

  • Gules - A red shield
  • Per fess sable and or - A black and gold shield divided horizontally.
  • Argent a cross gules - A silver shield with a red cross.
  • Paly vert and argent a chief azure a base azure - A green and silver vertically striped shield with a blue top and bottom.
  • Argent 3 roundels purpure - A silver shield with 3 purple circles.
  • Gules a lion rampant or armed argent and langued gules - A gold shield with gold lion on his hind legs with a red tongue and silver claws.
  • Purpure a chief or three bees volant proper - A purple shield with a gold upper section, 3 bees shown flying in their proper colours.
